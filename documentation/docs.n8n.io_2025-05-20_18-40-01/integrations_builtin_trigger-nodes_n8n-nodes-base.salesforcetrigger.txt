Salesforce Trigger node
#
Use the Salesforce Trigger node to respond to events in
Salesforce
and integrate Salesforce with other applications. n8n has built-in support for a wide range of Salesforce events.
On this page, you'll find a list of events the Salesforce Trigger node can respond to, and links to more resources.

Examples and templates
