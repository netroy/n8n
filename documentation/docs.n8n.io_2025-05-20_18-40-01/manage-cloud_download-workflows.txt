Download workflows
#
n8n Cloud instance owners can download workflows from the most recent backup.
You can do this with the
Cloud admin dashboard
.
How to download workflows
#
Log in to n8n
.
Select
Admin Dashboard
to open the dashboard.
In the
Manage
section, select the
Export
tab.
Select
Download Workflows
.
Accessing workflows after your free trial
#
You have
90 days
to download your workflows after your free trial ends. After that, all workflows will be
permanently deleted
and are unrecoverable.
