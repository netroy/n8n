crowd.dev Trigger node
#
Use the crowd.dev Trigger node to respond to events in
crowd.dev
and integrate crowd.dev with other applications. n8n has built-in support for a wide range of crowd.dev events, including new activities and new members.
On this page, you'll find a list of events the crowd.dev Trigger node can respond to and links to more resources.
