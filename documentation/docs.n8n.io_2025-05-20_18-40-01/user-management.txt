User management
#
User management in n8n allows you to invite people to work in your n8n instance. It includes:
Login and password management
Adding and removing users
Three
account types
:
Owner
and
Member
(and
Admin
for Pro & Enterprise plans)
Privacy
The user management feature doesn't send personal information, such as email or username, to n8n.
Setup guides
#
This section contains most usage information for user management, and the
Cloud setup guide
. If you self-host n8n, there are extra steps to configure your n8n instance. Refer to the
Self-hosted guide
.
This section includes guides to configuring
LDAP
and
SAML
in n8n.
