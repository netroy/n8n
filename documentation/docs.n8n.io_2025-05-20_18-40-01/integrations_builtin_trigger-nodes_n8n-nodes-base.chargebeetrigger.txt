Chargebee Trigger node
#
Chargebee
is a billing platform for subscription based SaaS and eCommerce businesses. Chargebee integrates with payment gateways to let you automate recurring payment collection along with invoicing, taxes, accounting, email notifications, SaaS Metrics and customer management.

Add webhook URL in Chargebee
#
To add a Webhook URL in Chargebee:
Open your Chargebee dashboard.
Go to
Settings
>
Configure Chargebee
.
Scroll down and select
Webhooks
.
Select the
Add Webhook
button.
Enter the
Webhook Name
and the
Webhook URL
.
Select
Create
.
